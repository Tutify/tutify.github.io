Privacy Policy

(Protecting your information while using Tutify)

What’s on this page?

This Privacy Policy explains how Tutify Pty. Ltd. (ABN: 32 614 010 614) (Tutify) collects, uses, maintains and discloses Personal Information collected by it including Personal Information collected from those who register as users of the http://www.tutify.com.au/ website (Site).

‘Personal Information’ in this privacy policy has the meaning given to it in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) (Act). In general terms, it is any information that can be used to personally identify you. This may include your name and contact details including phone numbers and email addresses.

By registering as a user of the Site, you consent to the collection and use of the information, including Personal Information you provide, in the manner we describe in this policy.

How we use your Personal Information

We will use information, including Personal Information you provide to us, to:

  • verify your identity when you register;
  • to enable our tutors to contact you for the purposes of providing their services;
  • facilitate and process other transactions that take place on the Site;
  • respond to support requests;
  • help facilitate the resolution of any disputes;
  • update you with operational news and information about the Site including website disruptions or security updates;
  • provide you with information that you request from us;
  • provide you with marketing information about products and services which we think may interest you;
  • carry out technical analysis to determine how to improve the Site;
  • monitor activity on the Site, including to identify potential fraudulent activity and to ensure compliance with the terms and conditions that apply to the Site;
  • manage our legal and operational affairs; and/or
  • provide general administrative and performance functions and activities.

We may disclose Personal Information to third parties in accordance with this policy and also where it is reasonably necessary in order to enable us to make the Site available to you, when we are required to do so by law or when you give us permission to do so.

We will not otherwise disclose your Personal Information to any other person.

For tutors, certain information will be disclosed to the public via your public profile to enable parents/students to find tutors. This includes:

  • Your profile picture
  • Your full name
  • Your most advanced qualification / education
  • Your suburb
  • Your interests
  • Your security checks (working with children and police check)

Should you not wish this information to be disclosed, you can choose to make your profile private so that it is not publicly visible.
