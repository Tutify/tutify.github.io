"Fixing a broken industry"

The tutoring industry in Australia is broken. Tutoring agencies fleece parents and exploit tutors . Parents are kept in the dark amd

At Tutify, our vision is to fix the tutoring in Australia. We use technology to train and assist tutors to deliver exceptional services to their clients. Our platform enables parents to find proven tutors immediately and streamlines the management of tutoring sessions so that tutors can focus on what they are being paid to do: tutor.

Our Team

Our team combines experience and deepexpertise across a broad range of areas to make our vision reality.

Richard Murray

Founder, CEO

Richard founded Tutify in 2016 in response to the issues of fragmentation in the tutoring industry in Australia. He has been a tutor for school students for 10 years, covering every conceivable age and subject. He is passionate about learning and teaching. A self-confessed ‘Student of the Brain’, he is currently also studying Neuroscience in his spare moments to better understand how we learn and how technological advances and research in brain science will affect education in the future.
